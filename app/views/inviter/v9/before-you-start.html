Before you start

Before you raise an application, you must provide the following contact information:

  • contact email address. This may be used to contact you about applications you have raised.
  • help for your applicants email address. This will be displayed to your applicants, so they can contact you or your team if they need support.

You will need to verify your contact information by entering a security code.

You can change your contact information later by going to Settings.
