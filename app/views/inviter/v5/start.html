Apply for Care ID

Use this service to:

  • give NHS staff and healthcare professionals access to NHS national systems
  • update Care ID applications you have submitted
  • check the progress of Care ID applications

Anyone who needs access to NHS national systems for patient information and data must have a Care ID with the right authorisation.

A Care ID will give people working in or with the NHS access to the information they need to do their job.

Start

Before you start

You will need Windows Hello, your Security Key or Smartcard with the right permissions and roles to manage Care ID applications.

You will be asked to enter the following information about the Care ID applicant into an online form:

  • name
  • work email address
  • mobile phone number
  • ODS code for applicant's organisation

By inviting this individual to apply for a Care ID, you are confirming that they have been through relevant employment checks with the organisation theyâ€™ll be working for.

What the applicant needs to provide

When you have submitted this information, the person who needs access to NHS national systems will be asked to complete the application.

They will need to provide their name, address, date of birth and National Insurance number on an online form and submit identity documents using a mobile app.

ID documents the applicant needs

2 photo IDs from this list:

  • UK or international passport
  • UK, EU or international driving licence
  • UK Biometric residence permit card
  • HM Armed Forces identity card

1 proof of address document from this list:

  • bank account or credit card statement, this must be from the last 6 months
  • mortgage statement, this must be from the last 6 months
  • utility bill, this must be from the last 6 months
  • council tax statement, this must be from the last 12 months

The applicant will be sent an email with instructions for downloading and using the app.
