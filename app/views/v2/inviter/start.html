NHS smartcard application

Use this service to:

  • apply for an NHS smartcard for colleagues or employees that need access to NHS systems
  • update and manage applications you have submitted
  • check the progress of applications

An NHS smartcard allows people working in or with the NHS to get information they need to do their job.

Start

Before you start

You will need your own NHS smartcard with roles or permissions that allow you to make a smartcard application for someone else.

You will be asked to enter the following information about the applicant:

  • name
  • email address
  • mobile number
  • your organisation's ODS number

You will need to ensure your organisation has completed DBS and right to work checks for the applicant.

What the applicant needs to provide

When you have submitted this information online, the person applying for the card will be asked to complete the application.

This will include an identity check via a mobile app. They will be sent an email with instructions for downloading and using the app.

The applicant will also need to provide:

  • national insurance number
  • passport or driving licence
  • bank or utility bill
