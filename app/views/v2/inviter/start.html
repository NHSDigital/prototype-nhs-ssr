Apply for a staff ID

Use this service to:

  • apply for access to NHS national systems for colleagues or employees
  • update and manage applications you have submitted
  • check the progress of applications

Anyone that needs access to NHS national systems for patient information and data requires the right authorisation.

This application will give people working in or with the NHS access to the information they need to do their job with a smartcard or a unique identity code.

Start

Before you start

You will need your NHS smartcard to begin the application process for someone else.

You will be asked to enter the following information about the applicant into an online form:

  • name
  • work email address
  • mobile number
  • organisation's ODS code

You will need to ensure the applicant’s organisation has completed DBS and right to work checks for the applicant.

What the applicant needs to provide

When you have submitted this information, the person that needs access to NHS national systems will be asked to complete the application.

This will include an identity check via a mobile app. They will be sent an email with instructions for downloading and using the app.

The applicant will also need to provide:

  • national insurance number
  • 2 forms of photo ID
  • 1 proof of address
  • take a photo of their face with a mobile for a face scan

Get advice and support

If you need help or support with the application you can contact your NHS Trust’s registration authority
