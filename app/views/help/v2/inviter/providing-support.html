Troubleshooting applicant issues

View our applicant help pages to troubleshoot common applicant queries and issues.

If an applicant is experiencing a technical issue which is preventing them from completing their application, you can go to the NHS Digital Customer Service Portal or email ssd.nationalservicedesk@nhs.net to raise the issue.

Include their application ID and full details of the issue, including mobile device type and OS version if this might be relevant.
