Apply for Care ID

A secure online ID verification service for NHS users to verify their identity online to get access to national systems and services.

Apply for Care ID can be used by Registration Authority (RA) managers, agents and sponsors as an efficient alternative to face-to-face registrations.

About this service

Health and care staff are invited to verify their identity online. Their applications are checked by an NHS England ID checker.

Once their identity has been verified, a profile will be created for them in Care Identity Service (CIS).

Who can use this service

Registration Authority (RA) managers, agents and sponsors will need Windows Hello, a Security Key or smartcard with one of these roles assigned:

  • R5080: Registration Authority manager
  • R5090: Registration Authority agent
  • B1300: Sponsor
  • R8008: Admin and clerical support worker
Start now

How it works

  1. Registration Authority (RA) managers, agents and sponsors send the applicant an invite.
  2. The applicant completes their application online. Find out more about what applicants need to apply
  3. The application is reviewed by NHS England ID checkers.
  4. If approved, a profile is created in Care Identity Service (CIS).
  5. Registration Authority (RA) managers and agents can then use Care Identity Management to add permissions and issue an authenticator, such as a smartcard.

Benefits

Apply for Care ID provides an efficient alternative to face-to-face registrations.

Benefits for applicants

Convenient

  • can be accessed anytime, anywhere.
  • reduces the need to travel for a face to face appointment or to pay for travel

Easy to use

  • can be used on a desktop, laptop or smartphone
  • verify an address by entering a UK address, reducing the need for physical proof of address documents
  • capture ID documents and upload a smartcard photo online

Timesaving

  • on average, applications take under 10 minutes to complete

Benefit for RA managers, RA agents and sponsors

Efficient

  • conduct significantly fewer face-to-face registrations
  • spend less time on the administrative burden of managing face-to-face registrations, freeing them up to concentrate on other jobs

Secure and trusted

  • identities are verified to a Good Practice Guide (GPG) 45 ‘High’ standard.
  • uses the latest biometric technology and removes any ambiguity regarding the validity of those identities.
