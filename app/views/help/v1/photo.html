Photo guidelines

Your photo must:

  • be clear and in focus
  • be taken against a plain, light coloured background
  • show you facing forwards and looking straight at the camera
  • show you with a plain expression and your mouth closed
  • have your head and shoulders visible
  • only include a head covering for religious or medical reasons
  • be suitable for a work setting

Your photo must not:

  • contain other people or objects in the background
  • have hair or other objects obscuring your face
  • have any shadows on your face or behind you
  • be edited by photo filters
  • contain sunglasses or tinted glasses
  • be a photo of a physical document, like a passport

How do I upload a photo that is on my phone, if I am using a computer

  1. Go to your phone’s gallery of photos and select the photo you want to use.
  2. Select an option to share this photo.
  3. Select an email option.
  4. The email should auto-populate with the photo attached and your email address as the sender, email the image to an email address that you can access on your computer.
  5. On your computer, you should find the email that you’ve sent to yourself with the photo attached, select the photo and download it to your computer.
  6. Return to the Apply for Care ID ‘Upload photo’ screen and it should enable you to find your recently downloaded photo in your computer files, often in the ‘Downloads’ section.
  7. If the online form has timed out, return to your invite email and begin the form again, using the same link.
