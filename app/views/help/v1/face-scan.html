How to scan your face using the mobile app

To scan your face successfully:

  1. hold your phone in front of your face
  2. place your face within the green frame

Make sure:

  • there is no direct light in your eyes or shadow on your face
  • you face forwards and place your face within the green frame
  • no one else appears in the background

The facescan took unexpectedly, what should I do

If your facescan is still clear enough to check that your face matches the face on the photo ID documents, then this should not be a problem.

If your facescan is not clear enough, then we may have to reject your application. In this case we'll let you know why, and you will have to repeat your application.

Which photo will be used on my smartcard

We will aim to use the photo that you have uploaded, as long as it meets professional and passport guidelines.

If this does not meet these guidelines, then we would aim to use the passport photo if captured from the biometric chip or a still image captured from the facescan.

If none of these presented are viable options for your smartcard photo, your application may be rejected and you will have to be re-invited to start your application again.
