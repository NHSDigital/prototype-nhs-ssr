How TransUnion verifies your UK address

We share your name, date of birth and UK address with a trusted organisation, TransUnion.

TransUnion are an information and insights company.

They check for connections between your address, name and organisations like:

  • banks
  • utility providers
  • local councils
  • electoral register

This check does not impact your credit score. Only you can see that this check has been done.
