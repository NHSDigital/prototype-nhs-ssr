My name does not match my photo IDs

If your given name or family name do not match your photo IDs, we may not be able to prove your identity using the online service.

Email [inviter-support-email] to continue.

Your email should include:

  • subject line: Apply for Care ID - my name does not match photo IDs
  • your application ID (4650349sc-28449347-ck340347-cn34834)
  • this description: I cannot complete my online application because my name does not match my photo IDs. I am asking for support to continue my application.

What happens next

The person managing your application will respond with instructions on how to continue your application, and:

  • you may need to attend a face to face meeting
  • you may be asked to provide other evidence of your name change, like a marriage certificate, a deed poll or a gender recognition certificate
