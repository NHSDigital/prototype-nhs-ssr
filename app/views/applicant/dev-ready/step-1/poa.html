Which proof of address document do you want to use?

You can provide a paper copy or an online statement.

You'll need to take a photo of this document, or upload a PDF, when proving your identity.

More information about what we will accept

We accept:

  • both paper and online statements - as a photo or PDF
  • statements from joint accounts if you are one of the named account holders
  • proof of address documents from outside of the UK

Photos of screens are not accepted.

If you use your driving licence as a proof of address you cannot use it as a photo ID
