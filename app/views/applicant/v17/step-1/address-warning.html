How TransUnion verifies your UK address

We share your name, date of birth and UK address with a trusted organisation, TransUnion.

TransUnion are an information and insights company.

They will check for connections between your address, name and organisations like:

  • banks
  • utility providers
  • local councils
  • electoral register
I have more than one UK address or have recently moved

Enter the address with the most connections to organisations like:

  • banks
  • utility providers
  • electoral register

If you have moved within the last couple of months:

  • provide your previous address if you are unlikely to have any activity connected to your name at your new address
  • or go back to the previous screen and verify your identity by providing a proof of address document for your new address and 2 photo IDs
