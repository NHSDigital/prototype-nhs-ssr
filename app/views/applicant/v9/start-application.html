Our checks showed that you do not already have a Care Identity profile. You will need to apply for one.

An email has been sent to you. You can use the link to return to start your application at another time.

What an NHS Care Identity is

An NHS Care ID will give you access national systems, clinical data and patient information.

You need an NHS Care ID to get an authenticator, such as a smartcard.

What you'll need to provide

  • your UK address for an online check, or a paper proof of address document
  • a photo ID, such as a passport or driving license
  • be prepared to take a passport style photo
  • a mobile phone with a camera and internet connection

The next part of the application will take about 10-15 minutes to complete.

You will then be prompted to download the secure Verify Care ID mobile app to photograph your ID document, take a passport style profile photo and scan your face.
