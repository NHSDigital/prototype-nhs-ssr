About this service

Staff can be invited to use this service to submit their identity documents and have them verified by an NHS Digital ID checker.

Once their identity has been verified, a profile will be created for them in Care Identity Service (CIS) and they will receive an authenticator, such as a smartcard, authenticator app, fob or device to enable them to access national systems and services.

Start now

Who is the service for

Apply for Care ID can be used by Registration Authority (RA) managers, agents and sponsors as an efficient alternative to face-to-face registrations.

Registration Authority (RA) managers, agents and sponsors will need Windows Hello, a Security Key or smartcard with one of these roles assigned:

  • R5080: Registration Authority manager
  • R5090: Registration Authority agent
  • B1300: Sponsor
  • R8008: Admin and clerical support worker

Key benefits

We recognise that verifying identities and issuing authenticators for large cohorts of students in a short timeframe presents a real challenge for Registration Authorities.

One particular use case for this product is to support with issuing student authenticators.

Apply for Care ID enables medical students to capture their own documents which are verified by a centralised team of NHS Digital ID checkers, if approved, this automatically creates a profile in Care Identity Service.

We have been working closely with the University of Birmingham and Oxford University teaching hospitals, amongst others, to issue smartcards to their students.

If you want to discuss student onboarding using Apply for Care ID, contact iamplatforms@nhs.net.
