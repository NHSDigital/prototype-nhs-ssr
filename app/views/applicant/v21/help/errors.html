I do not have a National Insurance number

If you do not have a National Insurance number, then you must contact the team managing your application. Their email address will be included in your invite email.

You may need to book a face-to-face meeting with them instead.

The service is telling me that I already have an NHS Care ID profile

The team managing your application will be able to see that we've found an existing profile and should contact you with next steps.

Alternatively, you could contact them, their email address will be included in your invitation email. You may need to book a face-to-face meeting for them to check and update your existing profile.

My application was unsuccessful

Check your emails, we’ll let you know that your application has not been successful and why, we’ll tell you how to resolve this for your next application.

Contact your the team managing your application, their contact email address will be included in your invitation and rejection email. They will need to send you a new application link for you to try again.
