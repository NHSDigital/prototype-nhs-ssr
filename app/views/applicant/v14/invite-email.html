You have been invited by your organisation to find out if you have an NHS Care ID profile.

You may already have an NHS Care ID if you worked for the NHS before or are moving to a new NHS Trust.

If you do not have a profile, you'll be invited to verify your identity to create one.

An NHS Care ID will give you access to national systems, clinical data and patient information.

You need an NHS Care ID profile to get an authenticator, such as a smartcard.

Use the short online form to check if you already have a profile.

You must complete this form by Friday 23 June 2023 5pm.

If you do not complete this form by the specified time you may need to request another invite email.

You'll need:

  • your full name
  • your date of birth
  • your National Insurance number
  • your mobile phone to receive a security code
