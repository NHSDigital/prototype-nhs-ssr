You need to apply for an NHS Care ID

Our checks showed that you do not already have a Care Identity profile. You will need to apply for one.

You can use the link in your invite email to return to start your application at another time.

How to apply

1. Complete the online form

  1. verify your current UK address
  2. select your ID document(s) for verification

2. Set up your NHS Care ID profile

  1. upload a passport style photo of your face
  2. enter name to appear on smartcard
  3. select a delivery address (if applicable)

These details will appear on your smartcard (if applicable).

3. Download the secure mobile app

  1. photograph your ID document(s)
  2. scan your face to verify your identity

I cannot use a mobile phone to take photos and scan my face

Start application

What is an NHS Care ID?

An NHS Care ID will give you access to national systems, clinical data and patient information.

You need an NHS Care ID to get an authenticator, such as a smartcard.
