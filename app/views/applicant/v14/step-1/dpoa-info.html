How your UK address is verified

We'll share your address, name and date or birth with a 3rd party organisation, TransUnion.

The system will check for connections between your address and organisations like banks, utility providers, local councils and the electoral register.

Only you will be able to see that this check has been done, and it will not impact your credit score.
