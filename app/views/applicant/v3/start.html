Apply for a staff ID

Use this service to verify your identity and apply for your staff ID.

Your staff ID will allow you to access the data and patient information you need for your role or job

To complete this application you will need to:

  1. confirm your personal details
  2. enter your national insurance number
  3. provide 2 forms of photo ID (valid passport, driving licence, EU identity card)
  4. provide 1 proof of address (recent utility bill, bank or mortgage statement)
  5. take a photo of your face with your mobile for a face scan

You need your mobile phone to receive a confirmation code to enter into the online application form.

You will also need your mobile phone to download and use the secure NHS ID verification app as part of your application.
