Apply for your Care ID

Dear...

This email is being sent to you to complete a secure NHS application for your Care ID.

You will need a Care ID if you are starting a new role or moving to a new NHS Trust. Your Care ID will allow you to access:

  • national systems
  • clinical data
  • patient information

To apply for a Care ID you will need to provide information about yourself and submit documents and photos to verify your identity. All of this can be done online by clicking on the “start application” button at the end of this email.

Your application needs to be completed by 12am Tuesday 22 February 2022. It will expire after this date and you will then need to request another application.

Documents you need for your application

2 photo IDs from this list:

  • UK or international passport
  • UK, EU or international driving licence
  • UK Biometric residence permit card
  • HM Armed Forces identity card
Information:

You will need original, physical documents and they must be current.

1 document from this list:

  • bank account or credit card statement, this must be from the last 6 months
  • mortgage statement, this must be from the last 6 months
  • utility bill, this must be from the last 6 months
  • council tax statement, this must be from the last 12 months
  • UK driving licence (if you are using your driving licence as a photo ID you cannot use it as proof of address)
Information:

You will need documents that have been sent to your address or printed and stamped by the service provider. Documents printed from online accounts will not be accepted.

Personal information you need to provide:

  • National insurance number
  • date of birth
  • postal address

How to submit your documents

You will need to download a secure mobile app on a mobile phone connected to the internet to submit your documents to be checked and verified.

A text message with a link to the mobile app will be sent to you when you have completed and submitted the first part of the online form.

Use the mobile app to photograph your ID documents and to take a photo of your face as part of the verification process.

You can only use the app on a mobile phone. The app will use about 60 MB and can be deleted once you have submitted the documents and taken the photograph of your face.
