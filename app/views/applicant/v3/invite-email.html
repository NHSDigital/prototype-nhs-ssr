Apply for your Care ID

Dear...

This email is being sent to you to complete a secure NHS application for your Care ID.

A Care ID may be a security code, card, fob or a device that will allow you to access:

  • national systems
  • clinical data
  • patient information

You will need a Care ID if you are starting a new role or moving to a new NHS Trust.

Your application needs to be completed by 12am Tuesday 22 February 2022. It will expire after this date.

Documents you need for your application

2 photo IDs from this list:

  • UK or international passport
  • UK, EU or international driving licence
  • UK Biometric residence permit card
  • HM Armed Forces identity card
Information:

You will need original, physical documents and they must be current.

1 document from this list:

  • bank account or credit card statement, this must be from the last 6 months
  • mortgage statement, this must be from the last 6 months
  • utility bill, this must be from the last 6 months
  • council tax statement, this must be from the last 12 months
  • UK driving licence (if you are using your driving licence as a photo ID you cannot use it as proof of address)
Information:

You will need documents that have been sent to your address or printed and stamped by the service provider. Documents printed from online accounts will not be accepted.

Additional information you need to provide:

  • National insurance number

How to submit your documents

You will need to download a secure mobile app called Verify Care ID on a mobile phone connected to the internet to submit your documents to be checked and verified.

A link to the Verify Care ID app will be sent to you as you progress through the application.

Start application

Contact enquiries@nhsdigital.nhs.uk for help and support using the app or verifying your identity.
