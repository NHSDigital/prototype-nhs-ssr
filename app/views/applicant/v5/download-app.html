Download the NHS Verify Care ID app

Information:

You have 15 days to complete your application using the app.

A link for the Verify Care ID app will be sent to you by text message to *********5314 and to the email address l********@n**.net.

You should receive this link within 5 minutes.

To verify your identity and complete the Care ID application use the link to download the secure NHS Verify Care ID app on a mobile phone.

Information:

The NHS Verify Care ID app can only be used from the link that has been sent to you. It will not work if you go to GooglePlay or the App Store without using the link.

You will use the Verify Care ID app to photograph your photo ID, proof of address documents and scan your face for a passport style photo.

Your mobile phone must have:

  • a camera
  • a connection to the internet

The Verify Care ID app can be deleted when your application has been approved.
