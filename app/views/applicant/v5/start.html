Apply for a Care ID

Use this service to verify your identity and apply for your Care ID.

Your Care ID will allow you to access the systems, data and patient information you need for your role.

Part 1: Enter your personal information

This must be completed by Friday 29 July 2022 9.02am

You will be asked to click on a button to send a security code to your mobile phone. Enter this code to continue.

You will then need to add some personal information, to check if your details are already in the NHS staff database:

  • your name as it appears on your photo ID
  • your date of birth
  • your National Insurance number
  • a delivery address to receive a Care ID card or other ID devices

Help and support with your application

Applying online and using the mobile app is the quickest way to verify your identity and get your Care ID.

Contact your manager, Registration Authority agent or application sponsor for help with your application.

Use the NHS Trust Registration Authority website to find your Registration Authority manager or agent.

Email enquiries@nhsdigital.nhs.uk for help and support using the website or app to verify your identity.
