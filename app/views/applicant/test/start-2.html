You need to apply for an NHS Care ID

Our checks showed that you do not already have a Care Identity profile. You will need to apply for one.

You can use the link in your invite email to return to start your application at another time.

What you'll need

  • your UK address for an online check, or a paper proof of address document
  • a photo ID, such as a passport or driving license
  • a mobile phone with a camera and internet connection

How to complete your application

  1. Complete an online form to verify your address and select your ID documents
  2. Download the secure mobile app to photograph your ID documents, take a passport style profile photo and scan your face

I cannot use a mobile phone to take photos and scan my face

Start application

What is an NHS Care ID?

An NHS Care ID will give you access national systems, clinical data and patient information.

You need an NHS Care ID to get an authenticator, such as a smartcard.
