You need to apply for an NHS Care ID

Apply for an NHS Care ID to access the systems, data and patient information you need for your role.

Once your application has been approved:

  • your NHS Care ID profile will be created
  • you will get an authenticator such as a smartcard, authenticator app, fob or device.

How to complete your application

  1. Select how you'd like to verify your address and identity
  2. Provide a passport style photo and name to appear on your NHS Care ID profile
  3. Verify your identity by downloading a mobile app and capturing your chosen documents and a face scan.

What you need

  • a photo ID, such as a passport
  • your UK address or a proof of address document
  • a mobile phone with a camera and internet connection

You may be asked for additional information, such as a delivery address to send an authenticator.
