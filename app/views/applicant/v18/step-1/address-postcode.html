I am a student living away from home, or have recently moved

Enter the address with the most connections to your name. For example:

  • banks
  • utility providers
  • electoral register

If you have moved within the last couple of months:

  • provide your previous address if you are unlikely to have any connections to your name at your new address
  • or verify your identity by providing a proof of address document for your new address and 2 photo IDs
I have more than 1 UK address or have recently moved

TransUnion will check for accounts registered in your name at your address. Accounts include banks, utility providers, or the electoral register.

If you have more than 1 UK address:

  • enter the address with the most accounts registered

If you have moved within the last couple of months:

  • enter your previous address if you are more likely to have accounts registered there

If you do not have accounts registered at your address:

  • verify your identity by providing a proof of address document instead
I have more than 1 UK address or have recently moved

TransUnion will check for connections between your address, name and organisations like banks, utility providers, or the electoral register.

If you have more than 1 UK address:

  • enter the address with the most connections

If you have moved within the last couple of months:

  • enter your previous address if you are more likely to have connections there

If you are unlikely to have any have connections to your UK address:

  • verify your identity by providing a proof of address document instead
