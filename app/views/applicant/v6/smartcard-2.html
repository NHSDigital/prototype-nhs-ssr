A photo is needed for your Care ID profile

The mobile app will take a photo of your face to appear on your Care ID profile.

This photo will appear on a smartcard if you are issued one.

Your photo must:

  • be clear and in focus
  • be taken against a plain, light coloured background
  • show you facing forwards
  • not contain other people or objects in the background
  • not have hair or other objects obscuring the face
